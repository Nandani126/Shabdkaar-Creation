Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A simple video might take 2-4 weeks, while a full feature film could take several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you work with clients outside of India?

Yes! We work with international clients and can accommodate remote collaboration. Travel arrangements can be made for on-location shoots when needed.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 30-50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Can you work with my existing content?

Absolutely! We can enhance existing footage, photos, or content. Many clients come to us for post-production work on content they've already captured.

What formats do you deliver in?

We deliver in multiple formats including HD, 4K, social media optimized, web-ready, and broadcast quality. We can also provide raw files if needed.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes, we include a set number of revisions in our packages. Additional revisions can be accommodated for a fee. We work collaboratively to ensure you're completely satisfied.
